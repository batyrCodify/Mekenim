Why Kyrgyzstan Is Special

Pearl of Central Asia

With its location in the heart of Central Asia, Kyrgyzstan seduces travelers with its majestic mountains, crystal clear lakes and rich culture. This pearl surprises with its pastoral landscapes, magnificent horse riding and the hospitality of the people. Climb the Tien Shan and discover the greatness of Kyrgyzstan.

Wealth of Culture and Greatness of Nature

Kyrgyzstan, located in Central Asia, delights with its richness of culture and the grandeur of nature. The Tien Shan mountain ranges and snow-covered peaks, the amazing Son-Kul and Issyk-Kul lakes, as well as the picturesque Kel-Suu and Suzak valleys will leave an unforgettable impression. Hospitable people, rich history and traditions make Kyrgyzstan unique and attractive for travelers.

Mountain Treasure and Cultural Heritage

Kyrgyzstan, a magnificent country of Central Asia, has an unsurpassed beauty of mountain landscapes and a rich cultural heritage. The majestic peaks of the Tien Shan, the amazing Issyk-Kul and Son-Kul lakes, as well as the picturesque Suzak and Kel-Suu valleys offer unforgettable adventures for travelers. The country is also famous for its hospitable people, folklore and traditional culture, making Kyrgyzstan a truly unique place to discover.
